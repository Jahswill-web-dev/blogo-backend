Guideline for Prompt (Sound Like Something You’d Ask a Friend)

Focus: Make the question feel personal, approachable, and peer-to-peer.

Instructions for AI:

Write as if you were asking a friend or colleague directly.

Use casual, friendly language — no formal or corporate tone.

Avoid jargon, stiff phrasing, or overly structured sentences.

Keep it relatable, warm, and human.

Maintain the original topic or intent while enhancing conversational tone.

Examples to guide AI:

❌ Stiff/Corporate: “Please share your top priorities when managing tasks.”

✅ Friendly/Conversational: “How do you usually decide what to tackle first?”
