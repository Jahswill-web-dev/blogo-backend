Subtype: Checklist / Do-This-Not-That
Goal: Give the audience a quick, scannable list they can immediately apply.

REQUIRED JSON SCHEMA:

  "title": "string (short title for the checklist)",
  "do": ["array of positive items (3–10 items the audience SHOULD do)"],
  "dont": ["array of negative items (3–10 items the audience should AVOID)"],
  "context_note": "string (explain why this checklist matters in the startup’s industry)",
  "cta": "string (short call-to-action to save, follow, or apply the checklist)"

WRITING NOTES:
- Keep items extremely concise (max one sentence).
- Use verbs (“Validate early…”, “Avoid guessing user intent…”).
- Ensure items relate to the startup’s product or ICP when possible.
