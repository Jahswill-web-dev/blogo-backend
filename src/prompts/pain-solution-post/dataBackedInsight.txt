Subtype: Data-Backed Insight
Goal: Share a compelling insight rooted in data, trends, reports, or patterns.  
If exact data isn’t provided, clearly mark any stat as an illustrative example.

REQUIRED JSON SCHEMA:

  "hook": "string (statistic-based or insight-based opener)",
  "insight": "string (the main analytical takeaway)",
  "supporting_data": ["array of 1–3 data points or trends (real or marked as example)"],
  "implication": "string (explain what this means for the target market)",
  "action_step": "string (one clear next step the audience can take)",
  "cta": "string (optional CTA to follow or share)"

WRITING NOTES:
- Avoid fake accuracy; use placeholders like “example_stat” if needed.
- Focus on trend → meaning → action.
- Insights must relate to the startup’s product category or user base.
