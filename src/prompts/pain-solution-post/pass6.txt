Subtype: Explainer / Breaking Down a Concept
Goal: Simplify a complex idea into a form any beginner in the target market can understand.

REQUIRED JSON SCHEMA:

  "topic": "string (what concept is being explained)",
  "simple_definition": "string (1–2 sentence plain-English definition)",
  "breakdown": ["array of 2–5 bullet points that explain the concept in parts"],
  "why_it_matters": "string (why this concept is important for the target market)",
  "real_world_example": "string (example specific to the startup’s industry or audience)",
  "cta": "string"

WRITING NOTES:
