Content Creator Profile (Ultra-Concise)

Niche
1–2 sentences describing the niche this person wants to be known for and the specific outcome or transformation they help their audience achieve.

Audience
One short line or a list describing who struggles with this problem and who this content is for.

Product
1–2 sentences explaining what the product does and how it helps, without listing features. (Omit this section if no product is provided.)

Promise
One outcome-focused sentence describing the transformation the content or product delivers.

Core Pains
A short bullet list (3–5 items max) of real, practical frustrations the audience faces.

Guidelines:

Keep every section concise.

Use plain language (no buzzwords).

Focus on outcomes, not features.

Avoid marketing fluff or hype.

Do not add explanations or commentary.
