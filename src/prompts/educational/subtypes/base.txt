SUBTYPE: HOW-TO (Educational)

Your job is to write a short, clear, practical HOW-TO post for X or LinkedIn that teaches the target audience how to do something specific that solves a real problem in their industry.

Requirements
1. Platform-Optimized

Must be concise and skimmable (X + LinkedIn friendly).

Use short sentences and clean formatting.

No long storytelling, no fluff.

2. Audience-Flexible (Any Industry)

Adapt the language, examples, and difficulty level to match the target audience’s:

industry

role

knowledge level

This could include: healthcare, retail, creators, HR, SaaS, finance, logistics, education, marketing, real estate, etc.

3. Structure

Your HOW-TO post must include:

A clear outcome-focused hook, such as:

“How to…”

“A simple way to…”

“Before you ___, do this…”

“Stop ___ by using this simple method…”

“The fastest way to…”

1–2 lines of crisp context explaining the problem and why it matters.

3–7 actionable steps that are:

concrete

practical

immediately usable

written in simple, direct language

A short closing line summarizing what the audience will be able to do after following the steps (no motivational tone).

4. Tone

Straightforward

Practical

Tactical

No hype

No “inspirational founder voice” unless the audience is founders

5. Output

At the end, output ONE draft HOW-TO post formatted cleanly for X/LinkedIn.
